--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sub-Clause 99.2.A: High-performance polymer extrusion requires strict control over 
extruder screw L/D (Length-to-Diameter) ratios, barrel cooling, and die head pressure.
- Single-Screw Extruders: Configured with a 30:1 L/D ratio for optimal melting.
- Zone 1 Heating: Controlled continuously to prevent premature resin melting.

- Cooling Lines: Must maintain a flow rate of 15 liters/minute at 2.5 bar pressure.

SECTION 1: PREAMBLE AND EXECUTIVE STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This document outlines the three-year strategic AI roadmap for Apex PolyPlast India Private Limited. We aim to integrate quantized Small Language Models (SLMs) into our operational workflows to secure proprietary design assets, optimize procurement pipelines, and improve industrial plant efficiency.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: AI DEPLOYMENTS MISSION STATEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To ensure complete data security, eliminate latency, and protect proprietary mold flow designs, Apex PolyPlast is executing a transition of its CRM and operational databases to on-premise, quantized SLMs, reducing reliance on public APIs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: PHASE 1: DEPARTMENTAL RAG SANDBOX INTEGRATIONS (CURRENT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Deploy sandboxed local RAG co-pilots for HR, Finance, and Legal divisions.
- Implement stopword-filtered keyword matching on local vector databases.
- Integrate manual file-upload interfaces in the CRM dashboard to index regional tax and policy guidelines.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: PHASE 2: LIVE EXTRUDER TELEMETRY AND EVENT BUS ROUTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Establish real-time WebSocket connections streaming machine temperature, CPU, and RAM vitals from our Silvassa Plant extruder heating loops.
- Connect department agents via a secure event bus to automate workflows like onboarding and vendor compliance audits.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: PHASE 3: HIGH-PERFORMANCE COMPUTE SCALING AND FINE-TUNING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Upgrade our Bengaluru R&D facility with a dedicated cluster of NVIDIA H100 servers.
- Perform custom fine-tuning of local models using our proprietary B2B interaction registries and polymer chemistry records, phasing out public API dependencies completely.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: RISK MANAGEMENT AND DATA EGRESS SAFEGUARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data security is maintained through:
- Automated scrubbing of direct messages and files for PII.
- Restricting access to model checkpoint configurations and vector stores to verified Level 5 clearance credentials.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: ON-PREMISE GPU SERVER NODE SPECIFICATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Our Bengaluru Whitefield hub will house:
- Four (4) NVIDIA H100 PCIe GPUs (80GB VRAM each) for high-performance localized modeling.
- Two (2) AMD EPYC 96-core processors with 512GB RAM, running vLLM inference pipelines.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
