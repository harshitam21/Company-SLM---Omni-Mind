ADDITIONAL OPERATIONS ANNEXURE: DETAILED INDUSTRIAL PLASTIC EXTRUSION MACHINERY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sub-Clause 99.2.A: High-performance polymer extrusion requires strict control over 
extruder screw L/D (Length-to-Diameter) ratios, barrel cooling, and die head pressure.
- Single-Screw Extruders: Configured with a 30:1 L/D ratio for optimal melting.
- Zone 1 Heating: Controlled continuously to prevent premature resin melting.
- Melt Temperature: Maintained at 240 deg C to ensure stable viscosity.
- Cooling Lines: Must maintain a flow rate of 15 liters/minute at 2.5 bar pressure.
Failure to maintain these thermal metrics will cause polymer degradation, resulting 
in machine downtime and expensive purging compound requirements.

SECTION 1: PREAMBLE & ONBOARDING PHILOSOPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To maintain high production standards and avoid industrial safety hazards, Apex PolyPlast India Private Limited mandates a rigorous, structured onboarding process for all newly joined personnel. Onboarding flows are split based on division to ensure that white-collar technical engineers receive proper system access and security certifications, while factory floor production labourers are fully trained in high-temperature extrusion safety and shift-handover protocols.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: DIVERGENT ONBOARDING PATHWAYS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Onboarding procedures must follow these distinct tracks:
- Track A (Technical / Design): Bengaluru R&D Center and Mumbai BKC HQ. Focuses on digital access, VPN setup, CAD tools, and cybersecurity certifications.
- Track B (Labour / Production): Silvassa Plant 1 and Sanand Plant 2. Focuses on physical safety, biometric gate access, extrusion nozzle thermal drills, and shift purging.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: TECHNICAL & DESIGN TEAM MILESTONE TIMELINE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Newly joined software engineers and design scientists must complete these milestones:
- DAY 1: WORKSTATION PROVISIONING & AD CREATION
  * Verification of Dell Precision laptop delivery.
  * Creation of Active Directory (AD) credentials and Google Workspace logins.
  * HR registration, Aadhaar data matching, and tax document uploads.
- DAY 3: VPN GATEWAYS & SIMULATION ENGINE SETUPS
  * Activation of the Apex-Shield Zero-Trust VPN Gateway.
  * Installation of Autodesk Moldflow, SolidWorks, and CRM database interfaces.
  * Verification of active login to the departmental AI co-pilot.
- DAY 14: MANDATORY ZERO-TRUST CYBERSECURITY TRAINING
  * Verification of clearance isolation boundaries (Level 1 to Level 5).
  * Completion of the SLM model weight protection tutorial.
  * Signed acknowledgment of corporate data protection agreements.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: R&D TOOL AND SIMULATION SOFTWARE INSTALLATION SOP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Bengaluru IT support must ensure the installation of the following technical suites on Day 3:
- Autodesk Moldflow: Configured with on-premise compute nodes for remote task rendering.
- SolidWorks: Synchronized with the global polymer materials database.
- CRM RAG Ingestion Interface: Enabled for document indexing up to Clearance Level 4.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: CYBERSECURITY ZERO-TRUST VERIFICATION TRAINING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
On Day 14, technical employees must complete the Zero-Trust security module. The system will automatically freeze VPN access if the employee fails to score 100% on the security test within two weeks of onboarding.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: PLANT LABOUR TEAM MILESTONE TIMELINE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Labourers and machine operators in Silvassa and Sanand plants must complete these milestones:
- DAY 1: BIOMETRICS, CONTRACT VERIFICATION, & PF SETUP
  * Biometric fingerprint scanner registration at the factory gates.
  * Submission of EPFO / ESIC registration files.
  * Handout of raw materials handling guidelines.
- DAY 3: EXTRUSION NOZZLE HAZARD DRILLS
  * Direct training on high-temperature operations (screw and die zones).
  * physical extruder emergency shutdown drills.
- DAY 7: SHIFT HANDOVER PROCEDURES & PPE CERTIFICATION
  * Training on Shift Handover Protocols (SHP).
  * Quality fitment of steel-toed boots, helmets, and thermal gloves.
  * Signing of the Factories Act, 1948 compliance sheet.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: MANDATORY POLYMER EXTRUSION & SAFETY DRILLS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
On Day 3, plant operators must execute a practical emergency drill. They must demonstrate the ability to cut power to the extruder screw within three (3) seconds of a simulated jam to prevent fire hazards from overheated polymer melt (240°C).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8: SHIFT HANDOVER PROTOCOL TRAINING (SHPT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Improper shift changes cause polymer to solidify, causing massive damage:
- Zone Purge: Outgoing operators must demonstrate proper purging of extruder zones.
- Flow Check: Incoming operators must verify cooling water line pressure and check zone temperatures.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9: BIOMETRIC GATE REGISTRATIONS AND PF ENROLMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Day 1 biometric registration is non-negotiable. Fingerprint scanners at the plant gates determine shift attendance and automatically calculate ESIC sick-leave allocations.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 10: APPENDIX A - TECHNICAL ONBOARDING ACKNOWLEDGMENT SHEET
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every technical employee must co-sign the digital CRM onboarding card:
- "I certify that my workstation is secure, my Zero-Trust VPN credentials are active, and I have completed the SLM security clearance tutorial."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 11: APPENDIX B - LABOUR SAFETY COMPETENCE TEST (BIS STANDARDS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Prior to operating Plant extruders, all operators must pass a physical safety test:
- PP melting zone check: Pass/Fail.
- Die zone pressure check: Pass/Fail.
- Steel-toed boot inspection: Pass/Fail.
All results are filed in the HR CRM database.
