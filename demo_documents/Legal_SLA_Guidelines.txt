SECTION 3: AUTOMATED B2B ORDER DISPATCH API PARAMETERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The B2B order dispatch system automates procurement and shipping operations:
- Order Dispatch API: Connects regional logistics centers to plant warehouses.
- Real-Time Tracking: Provides status updates for freight dispatch logs.
- Automatic Validation: Verifies GSTIN details and HSN matching.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: DISRUPTION PENALTY CREDIT CALCULATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If the B2B order portal experiences an outage, service penalty credits will accumulate at five percent (5%) of monthly enterprise system access fees per hour of downtime, applied to the partner's subsequent monthly billing cycle in Indian Rupees (INR).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: DATA LEAK AND PRIVACY BREACH CLAUSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Standard liability limitations and caps do not apply in cases of security clearance bypass or direct PII leakage from customer CRM databases, which will result in immediate system suspension and legal review under Indian information security laws.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: MAINTENANCE EXCLUSIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Outages resulting from regional internet gateway blackouts, state-directed fiber cutoffs, or pre-scheduled system upgrades (communicated 48 hours in advance) will be excluded from uptime computations.
