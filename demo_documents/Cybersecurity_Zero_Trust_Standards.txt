ADDITIONAL OPERATIONS ANNEXURE: DETAILED INDUSTRIAL PLASTIC EXTRUSION MACHINERY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sub-Clause 99.2.A: High-performance polymer extrusion requires strict control over 
extruder screw L/D (Length-to-Diameter) ratios, barrel cooling, and die head pressure.
- Single-Screw Extruders: Configured with a 30:1 L/D ratio for optimal melting.
- Zone 1 Heating: Controlled continuously to prevent premature resin melting.
- Melt Temperature: Maintained at 240 deg C to ensure stable viscosity.
- Cooling Lines: Must maintain a flow rate of 15 liters/minute at 2.5 bar pressure.

in machine downtime and expensive purging compound requirements.

SECTION 1: PREAMBLE AND SECURITY STANDARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This document establishes the security standards and protocols for Apex PolyPlast India Private Limited. We aim to secure our corporate information architecture and protect telemetry systems from malicious exploitation, in compliance with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, and CERT-In guidelines.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: ZERO-TRUST ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Our system operates under the principle of "never trust, always verify." All computational requests, database queries, and system transactions must undergo multi-factor authentication and clearance check.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: LOCAL SLM MODEL WEIGHT ISOLATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Access to local SLM weights directories, model checkpoints, and design databases strictly requires Level 5 security clearance. File access must be verified through signed JWT tokens.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: TELEMETRY NODE PROTECTIONS (EXTRUDERS & COOLING COILS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Real-time thermal sensors on our Silvassa extruder heating coils operate at 240°C. Unauthorized access or injection commands targeting extruder controllers will trigger an automatic security lock, protecting physical plant systems from malicious interference.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: INDIAN PII SCRUBBING AND DATA MASKING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To comply with Indian IT Regulations (Section 43A of the IT Act, 2000), our secure cybersecurity agent scrubs all sensitive data parameters before RAG indexing:
- Aadhaar Numbers (12-digit national IDs)
- Permanent Account Numbers (PAN)
- Employee bank IFSC codes and account details
- Indian phone numbers (+91)
- Medical and health details linked to ESIC plant rosters.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: CRYPTOGRAPHIC CREDENTIALS & JWT TOKENS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
All internal CRM transactions and API calls must include valid signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT) containing granular security clearances.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: IMMUTABLE DATABASE LOGS AND AUDITING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
All access attempts to secure model weights or directories are logged in an encrypted, off-site Postgres database for auditing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8: THREAT MODELING AND ANOMALY DETECTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The cybersecurity system uses anomaly detection algorithms to identify suspicious activities, such as multiple high-clearance access attempts from unknown IP addresses, triggering automatic account freezes.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9: REGULATORY COMPLIANCE WITH INDIAN IT ACT, 2000
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Apex PolyPlast maintains compliance with the IT Act, 2000, and CERT-In reporting guidelines, ensuring prompt reporting of cybersecurity incidents within the mandated timeframes.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Definition of corporate access levels:

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11: APPENDIX B - ANONYMIZATION REGEX RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sample regex configurations utilized by the Anonymization Engine:
- Aadhaar Scrubbing: `^[2-9]{1}[0-9]{3}\s[0-9]{4}\s[0-9]{4}$` (replaces with `[AADHAAR_MASKED]`).
- PAN Scrubbing: `^[A-Z]{5}[0-9]{4}[A-Z]{1}$` (replaces with `[PAN_MASKED]`).
